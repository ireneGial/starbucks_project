Our Starbucks Voucher System was created to help our customers in need by giving out a certain amount of coupons so you can keep enjoying our services .

Steps to apply for a chance to get a coupon:

1) Fill out an application form by clicking on "Apply for Coupon " on Voucher's homepage.
Careful : All applications must be completed before (expiration date)

2) After the expiration date has passed , check out our MIS System to see if you have been selected

3) If you see your AMKA in the "Selected Members" list come back here and log into your account.

4) Find you coupon code in your profile.

5) Copy it and from main menu click the option "Redeem Code".

6) Print your coupon and use it!

NOTE : The coupon is of one use, so even if you print the coupon more than once you will not be able to use it again.
